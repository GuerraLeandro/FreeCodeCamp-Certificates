Tribute to Olavo de Carvalho

Olavo de Carvalho, one of Brazil's most influential contemporary philosophers.

Olavo de Carvalho (1947–2022) was a Brazilian philosopher, writer, and political thinker. Known for his profound analysis of contemporary culture and politics, his works deeply impacted generations with reflections on philosophy, religion, and society.
